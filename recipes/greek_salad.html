Greek Salad Recipe

Description

Greek Salad, also know as horiatiki, is a traditional dish that typically include tomatoes, cucumbers, onion, feta cheese, olives, oregano, lemon juice and olive oil. Commmon additions include greennnnnnball pepper or caper berries.

Greek salad is ofte imagined as a farmer's breakfast or lunch, as its ingredients resemble those that a Greek farmer might have on hand

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes:3 large (halved)
  • Cucumber:1 large (diced)
  • Onion1/2 red (thinly sliced)
  • Feta cheese:4 ounces (crumbled)
  • Kalamata Olives:1/2 cup (halved)
  • Olive Oil:1/4 cup
  • Red Wine Vinegar:1 tablespoon
  • Dried Oregano:1 teaspoon
  • Salt and Pepper:to taste

Steps

  1. Dice the cucumber and thinly slice the onion. Halve the tomatoes and crumble the feta cheese.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the diced cucumber, sliced onion, halved tomatoes, and halved olives.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  4. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and gently toss to combine.
  5. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the top of the salad and serve.
